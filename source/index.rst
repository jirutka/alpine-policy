Alpine Policy Manual
====================

This manual describes the policy requirements for the Alpine Linux distribution,
including the structure and contents of the Alpine Linux repositories, packaging
policy, the design and technical issues surrounding the APK package manager and
various other design and technical requirements that are required for packages
to be included in the Alpine Linux distribution.
